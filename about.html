About Us

Ares aims to be a market leader in luxury watches. Founded as a small watch shop in 2000, we want to make the highest quality watches for our customers to be proud of wearing.


Company Timeline

2017

Ares becomes leading brand of watches, with sales of 5 million.

2015

Ares collaborates with RMAF (Royal Malaysian
Air Force) to create special editions of Ares
watches.

2013

Ares launches new line of LED watches.

2011

Sales of Ares watches hit 1 million.

2003

Ares stops producing pocket watches
due to low sales.
Begins development and production
of automatic chronograph watches.

2001

Ares launches its first pocket watch.

2000

Ares is founded in Kuala Lumpur.
